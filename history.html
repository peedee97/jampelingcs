Brief History

The present Jampeling Central School, is an off-shoot of the erstwhile Kanglung Lower Secondary School in the begining of 2005 academic session The name Jampeling was christened by His Holiness, the present 70th Jekhenpo (Trulku Jigme Choeda) for wellbeing and prosperity of the schoool community. It was officially declared in the beginning of 2012 academic session with prior permision from the Ministry of education Skills and Development vide office order NO.DSE/SLCD/2012/709 dated 31st May, 2012. It was upgraded to Higher Secondary School in 2011 with the introduction of one section of science stream. At present we have classes from VII to XII offering Science and Arts stream.

It is located 22 kilometers away up from the Dzongkhag Headquaters, trashigang below highway to Samdrupjongkhar when about to reach Kanglung satellite town. The bird eye view of the school from sign board on the main road looks all the infrastructure are well planned and constructed across the wide spread area of pristine natural surrounding covered with different species of flora and founa. The school has perhaps, one of the spacious compounds with 43 acres making it enjoyable learning culture and climate. The site view scenic beauty and a most enjoyable spot have always made attraction dor the visitors. It is a high valued GNH school with beautiful campus and site Development.

This school serves students from the catchment area of Kanglung satellite town of Goverment servants, business and local community of Rongthong, Pangthang, Ritshangdung, and Yonphula. The schools emphasized on good academic performance as we have good rapport of academic excellence every year in the Board Examination. All activities in the school are geared towardes the all round development of the productive citizens of the country. The school has as extensive agriculture practices with strong stressed to indicates dignity of labour in students.
